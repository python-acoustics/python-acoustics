Ambisonics¶

acoustics.ambisonics.acn(order=1)[source]¶

Spherical harmonic degree n and order m for ambisonics order order.

Parameters:order – Ambisonics order.

Yields tuples (n, m) where n is the degree and m the order.

Follows ACN.

ACN n m letter
0 0 0 W
1 1 -1 Y
2 1 0 Z
3 1 +1 X
